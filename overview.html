Rexos Cycle is a solid waste recycling company in Nigeria that was incorporated to solve the issues of solid waste management that has bedeviled our society. At Rexos Cycle we believe that commitment,Team work and innovation can take us to the Zenith.

Mission

Our mission is to Ensure that solid waste is managed properly in the environment by reducing the reliance on Landfills through reuse and recycling programs. To accomplish this mission we engage consumers using innovative techniques that foster growth and Development.

Vision

To protect the environment and promote proper hygiene through innovation in solid waste Recycling that meets industry standards.
